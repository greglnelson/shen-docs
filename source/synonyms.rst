.. _synonyms:

Synonyms
========


Synonyms allow the use of shorthands for types. All types are normalised to their definiens. ::

  (12+) (synonyms coor (number * number))
  synonyms : symbol

  (13+) (@p 1 2) : coor
  (@p 1 2) : (number * number)
